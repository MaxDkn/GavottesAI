About the Project

HomePredictorAI is an innovative tool designed to enhance your door-to-door sales strategies by leveraging artificial intelligence. Our goal is to predict the likelihood of a sale based on various home attributes, allowing you to focus your efforts more effectively.

Whether you're selling for a school project, a community initiative, or a business venture, HomePredictorAI provides data-driven insights to make your sales approach smarter and more efficient.
